About

The idea of making this website was taken directly from the LaLaLand movie and its famous jazz club Seb’s. All the information about the club itself and the musicians were made up. Though the fans of LaLaLand will surely find a grain of truth in it.
Since it was one of my first website ever made, this project aimed to put my skills into practice. What is more, it is fully responsive and mobile adjusted.
